FAQ

Common questions

Do I need an API key to use Validator?
+
Yes — Validator calls AI APIs directly from your browser. Groq and Google Gemini have free tiers, so you can get started at zero cost. Get a Groq key at console.groq.com — takes about 2 minutes.
Where is my data stored?
+
Everything — JDs, CVs, evaluations, messages, notes — is stored in your browser's localStorage only. It never touches any Validator server. Your API key is sent directly to your chosen AI provider (Anthropic, OpenAI, etc.).
Which AI provider gives the best results?
+
Anthropic's Claude Sonnet 4 consistently delivers the most accurate JD analysis and CV scoring. For free tiers, Groq's Llama 3.3 70B and Google Gemini 2.0 Flash are both excellent. You can switch providers at any time in Settings.
Can multiple recruiters use the same Validator?
+
Yes. Each recruiter registers their own account with their own API key. The Admin panel lets you manage all users, view activity, and export data. Each user can choose their own AI provider independently.
What is the Challenge feature?
+
AI models can hallucinate or produce results that don't match your context. The Challenge button on every AI result lets you flag what's wrong — e.g. "score seems too high, CV lacks evidence of team leadership." Unchallenged results are automatically marked Accepted, creating a full audit trail of every evaluation decision.
How accurate is the CV scoring?
+
Validator uses the same JD analysis and skill matrix for every candidate against a role — ensuring consistent relative scoring. The AI evaluates depth of evidence, ownership, and outcomes rather than keyword presence. All scores are decision-support tools and should be reviewed by a human before any hiring decision.
How does the Outreach feature work?
+
Validator generates personalised messages and opens them in your default email client (via mailto:) or WhatsApp Web. Your messages are sent through your own accounts — Validator never sends on your behalf. All communication logs are saved in your browser only.
Can I export my data?
+
Yes. Settings → Export Backup saves all JDs, candidates, evaluations, and messages as a JSON file. Admins can also export candidate scores as a CSV from the Admin panel. Import backups at any time to restore your data.
