How It Works

From JD to hire in
three steps

No complex setup. No training required. Just paste your JD and CV, and let the AI do the heavy lifting.

01

Analyse the Job Description

Paste any JD and Validator extracts hiring intent, seniority expectations, must-haves, and hidden signals — building a weighted 100-point scoring framework automatically.

02

Score the Candidate CV

Paste the CV and the AI scores each competency — inferring depth, ownership, and outcomes rather than keyword matching. Every score comes with a justification.

03

Compare & Decide

Compare any number of evaluated candidates side-by-side. Get a panel-ready report with ranked recommendations, risk assessments, and interview strategy per candidate.

A note on AI and hiring

Validator is an AI-assisted tool. All outputs — scores, recommendations, and evaluations — are generated by a large language model and must be reviewed by a qualified human professional before being used in any hiring decision. Validator does not make hiring decisions; it helps you make better ones. Users are responsible for ensuring their use complies with applicable employment and anti-discrimination laws.
